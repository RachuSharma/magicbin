Plastic

What belongs in the plastic bin?
Important
Emptied and cleaned · Place loosely in bin — not in a bag
Plastic
Plastic trays (all colours including black)
Plastic bottles
Plastic tubs
Plastic bags (without metallic film)
Plastic wrap and film
Bubble wrap
Plastic containers
Plastic toys (without electronics)
Plastic lids
Plastic tableware (not polystyrene)
Beverage & Food Cartons
Milk cartons
Yoghurt cartons
Juice cartons
Drink cartons
Food cartons e.g. chopped tomatoes, sauces and beans
Food cartons: Empty, scrape clean and fold flat before placing in bin.
